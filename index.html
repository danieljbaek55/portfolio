I believe in designing for function: systems that integrate seamlessly into user workflows at a fundamental level, not ones that demand behavioral change. My practice is built on co-creation with users, leaning on their domain expertise to surface the experiences worth building.

I believe the craft of design is evolving. I actively leverage AI tools to expedite idea generation, synthesis, and prototype creation, reserving deep craft skills for refinement, judgment, and the nuance that makes work land.
