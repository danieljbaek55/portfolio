Overview

The Problem

Intuit's domain experts carry deep procedural knowledge — the kind that lives in their heads, not in documentation. When an expert walks through a complex task, that process is invisible to the rest of the organization and impossible to scale.

Atlas is a behavior learning capability that changes this. It captures screen activity and transcript data as an expert works, then structures that behavior into a reusable skill — a discrete, deployable unit of knowledge that other experts and AI systems can draw on within Intuit's internal platform.
